ABOUT MAX SEBOLD

Max Sebold is a New York based photographer, specializing in highly stylized, conceptual photography emphasizing digital compositing and manipulation. He studies photography at the School of Visual Arts in New York City, and currently works as a freelance photographer and retoucher. His work transforms reality into a surrealistic fantasy, an outlet for creativity and imagination. The very foundations of matter and space are visibly shattered, and reality is reduced to mere perception. Feel free to enter his world, a world shrouded in mystery, where the impossible is believably possible, and innovation is pushed to infinite boundaries
